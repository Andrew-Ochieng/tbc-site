Contact Us

Our meeting place is in the Greenfields Estate of the Eastlands area of Nairobi, next to Donholm Primary School, about 10km East of the city centre, 20 minutes drive to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. If you are coming by car turn off the Outer Ring Road at the Caltex petrol station, then turn second left by the Selfridges Supermarket, go past the School and Trinity Baptist Church is on your right after the first right. If you are coming by public transport take matatu 34B (destination written as Donholm, Savanna, Greenfields) from the top of Accra Road in the town centre and you will be dropped right by the church gates.
